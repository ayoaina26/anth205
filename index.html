  • Supplemental Instruction (SI)

    We are fortunate to have an excellent Supplemental Instruction (SI) leader this semester, Mr Garrett Garvelli. SI is a program run out of the Center for Teaching Excellence that provides an SI leader for certain courses in the university (typically large intro courses). An SI leader for a course is a student who has excelled in the course in the recent past and now shares their expertise by offering regular help sessions for current students of the course. Typically, your SI instructor will offer 2 or 3 regularly scheduled help sessions each week. The program is completely free. Its purpose is solely to help improve student performance. Take advantage of it! Statistically, students who regularly attend SI do significantly better (up to a full letter grade) than their classmates who do not. The SI session schedule can be found on the syllabus.
